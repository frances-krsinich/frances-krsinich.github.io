Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

My focus for Taha Hinengaro will be looking after my emotional and mental wellbeing by using my morning walk as a time for both mindfulness and letting my thoughts wander. So I don't listen to podcasts or music but just be. I find this is one of the most effective ways I can unwind and de-stress. Also, I will meditate for at least 10 minutes a day, and keep a daily journal. But probably most important, my communication and relationship with my wife helps to keep me emotionally and mentally balanced and healthy, and doing fun things together (usually involving eating food and visiting op-shops) is restorative!
